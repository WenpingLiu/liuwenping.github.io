Short Bio:

Wenping Liu is currently a part-time Postdoc at School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, Hubei, under the supervision of Dr. Xiaofei Liao. From Dec., 2014 to Dec., 2015, he visited Prof. Jiangchuan Liu at School of Computing, Simon Fraser University, Canada, supported by CSC. He received the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree in maths, in 1999 and 2004, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Communication Engineering and Information System, in 2012, all from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. He is working with Dr. Hongbo Jiang and currently his research interests focus on wireless networks, mobile computing and internet of things.
