Short Bio:

Wenping Liu is currently a part-time Postdoc in School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, Hubei, under the supervision of Dr. Xiaofei Liao. He received the B.S. degree and the M.S. degree in maths, in 1999 and 2004, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in Communication Engineering and Information System, in 2012, all from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. He is working with Dr. Hongbo Jiang and his research interests focus on the topology recognition and its applications in 2D/3D sensor networks.
