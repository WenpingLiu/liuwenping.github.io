Short Bio:

Wenping Liu is currently a Full Professor (with University Professorship, 腾龙学者) at the School of Information Management and Statistics, Hubei University of Economics, Wuhan, China, and leads the Mobile Computing and Business Intelligent (MoBI) group. He was a part-time Postdoc at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, Hubei, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaofei Liao. Before that he obtained his Ph.D. degree in Communication Engineering and Information System from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2012, under the supervision of Prof. Wenyu Liu and Prof. Hongbo Jiang. From Dec., 2014 to Dec., 2015, he visited Prof. Jiangchuan Liu at the School of Computing, Simon Fraser University, Canada, supported by CSC. His research interests focus on wireless networks, mobile computing and internet of things.
